City Council Meeting Agendas

COUNCIL MEETINGS

Regular meetings of the Madera City Council are held the 1st and 3rd Wednesday of each month at 6:00 p.m. in the Council Chambers at City Hall located at 205 W. 4th St., Madera, California 93637.

AGENDAS

Meeting agendas are posted on the bulletin board at City Hall at least 72 hours (generally Friday) prior to the meeting and to the website no later than Monday prior to the meeting. Questions regarding meeting agendas? Contact the Madera City Clerk at (559) 661-5405.
