American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009
The American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 (ARRA) was signed into law by President Obama on February 17, 2009. The ARRA awards $980 million in Community Development Block Grant-Recovery (CDBG-R) funds through the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD). To distribute these funds to cities, counties, insular areas and states, HUD has suggested a format for grantees to follow.
Our CDBG-R grant amount is $260,115.
